INSTAGRAM CAROUSEL — SUMMIT RECAP + CHIPOS DISCOVERY
======================================================

10-SLIDE CAROUSEL: 6 photos + 4 designed text cards
People come for the summit highlights. They discover ChipOS.
Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter — same slides.

FORMAT: "Summit Recap" storytelling carousel (2026 best practice)
  - Mixed media: photos for authenticity, text cards for data
  - Day-by-day structure = easy to follow, high save rate
  - ChipOS enters on Day 3 as ONE of the announcements, not the
    whole post. It's news, not an ad.
  - 10 slides = sweet spot for educational/story carousels

WHY THIS WORKS:
  - People SAVE summit recaps (algorithm gold)
  - People SHARE news roundups (extends reach)
  - ChipOS is DISCOVERED, not pitched
  - You deliver VALUE first (4 slides of news) before any
    product mention


DESIGN SYSTEM
--------------------------------------------
Photo slides:
  - Your photo with dark gradient overlay
  - White bold text, left-aligned, top 60%
  - Cyan accent (#00FFFF) for key numbers and names

Text card slides:
  - Dark background (#0a0a0f — your brand color)
  - India tricolor accent line at top (saffron → white → green)
  - "DAY X" header in cyan
  - Arrow bullets (→) in cyan, content in white
  - Clean, data-viz feel — NOT a PowerPoint

All slides:
  - 1080 x 1350 px (4:5)
  - Font: Montserrat Bold / Inter Bold
  - Slide counter bottom-right (1/10, 2/10...)

DESIGN SPECS
=====================================================================

Dimensions: 1080 x 1350 px (4:5 ratio)

PHOTO SLIDES (1, 3, 5, 7, 9, 10):
  - Your photo with dark gradient overlay
  - Overlay: linear-gradient(180deg,
    rgba(0,0,0,0.2) 0%, rgba(0,0,0,0.75) 100%)
  - Text: white, left-aligned, top 60%
  - Keep your face visible — people engage with people

TEXT CARD SLIDES (2, 4, 6, 8):
  - Background: #0a0a0f (your brand dark)
  - India tricolor accent line at top: 4px bar,
    gradient #FF9933 → #FFFFFF → #138808
  - "DAY X" headers: cyan (#00FFFF), Orbitron font 50pt
  - Arrow bullets (→): cyan
  - Content: white (#FFFFFF) bold 30-36pt
  - Sub-descriptions: off-white (#E5E5E5) 24-28pt
  - Consistent spacing between items

Typography:
  - Headlines: Montserrat Bold or Inter Bold, 44-80pt
  - Body: Montserrat Medium, 28-36pt
  - Accent: Cyan (#00FFFF) for numbers, names, CHIPOS, DAY X
  - Slide counter: 18pt, bottom-right, 40% opacity

Swipe Arrow: Slide 1 only, bottom-right, 30% opacity
