Frequently Asked Questions

Is Savitri a replacement for human therapy?+

No. Savitri is an AI wellness companion that uses evidence-based therapeutic techniques for daily mental health support. It's designed to complement, not replace, professional mental health care. If you're in crisis, please contact emergency services.

What therapy methods does Savitri use?+

Savitri offers three evidence-based approaches: CBT (Cognitive Behavioral Therapy) for challenging negative thought patterns, DBT (Dialectical Behavior Therapy) for emotional regulation, and ACT (Acceptance and Commitment Therapy) for building psychological flexibility.

Is my data private and secure?+

Yes. All conversations in Savitri are end-to-end encrypted. We never sell your data to third parties. You can export or delete all your data at any time. Our security practices are HIPAA-informed.

What platforms is Savitri available on?+

Savitri is available on iOS and Android. Download from the App Store or Google Play Store to start your wellness journey.
