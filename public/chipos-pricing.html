FREQUENTLY ASKED

What is an App Pack?

App Packs are domain-specific extensions that add specialized capabilities to ChipOS. For example, the Voyager SDK App Pack adds AI hardware acceleration features for Axelera Metis chips. Each tier includes a certain number of App Pack slots where you can install purchased packs.

What happens if I exceed my quota?

When you reach 80% of any quota, you'll receive a warning notification. At 100%, the feature is paused until the next billing cycle or until you purchase an overage pack. You can buy overage packs at any time to continue working without interruption.

Can I switch between plans?

Yes! You can upgrade at any time and the change takes effect immediately. When downgrading, the change takes effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Unused quota does not roll over between months.

Is there a free trial for paid plans?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial of the Pro plan with full features. No credit card required to start. For Enterprise trials, please contact our sales team to discuss your specific requirements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) through Stripe. Enterprise customers can also pay via invoice with NET-30 terms. Annual plans can be paid upfront or quarterly.
