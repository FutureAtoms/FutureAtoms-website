Core Features

Knowledge Management

Store and search through documentation, code, and resources. Crawl websites, upload PDFs, and organize your knowledge base.

Smart Search with RAG

Advanced retrieval-augmented generation with intelligent context retrieval for your AI coding assistants.

Task Management

Integrated task tracking with AI assistance. Collaborate with your coding assistant on tasks in real-time.

MCP Server

Model Context Protocol server for seamless integration with Claude Code, Cursor, Windsurf, and more.

Real-Time Updates

Live synchronization as you add new content and collaborate with your coding assistant.
