Frequently Asked Questions

Does Swaastik replace doctors?+

No. Swaastik is an AI healthcare data platform that automates data entry, digitizes records, and coordinates care across facilities. Clinical decisions remain entirely with qualified doctors.

How does the prescription OCR work?+

Our AI uses CNN-LSTM neural networks trained on 50,000+ Indian prescriptions to achieve 94.7% accuracy on handwritten text. The doctor writes normally, the system processes the image in 30 seconds, and the doctor verifies with a single tap.

Is patient data secure?+

Yes. All data is encrypted with AES-256 end-to-end encryption. We are ABDM-compliant and follow the DISHA framework. Data residency is maintained in India. Patients control consent for data sharing.

Which facilities can use Swaastik?+

Swaastik is designed for India's entire public health infrastructure: Sub-Centers, PHCs, CHCs, District Hospitals, and Medical Colleges. Private hospitals can also integrate through our standard APIs.
