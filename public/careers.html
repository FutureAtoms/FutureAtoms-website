FREQUENTLY ASKED

Questions candidates often ask us.

What's the interview process like?

Our process is designed to respect your time. It typically includes: (1) Initial chat with a team member to learn about your background and interests, (2) Technical conversation or take-home project based on your preference, (3) Final conversation with the founder. The whole process usually takes 1-2 weeks.

What stage is the company at?

We're early-stage and self-funded. We have multiple products in market with real users. This is a ground-floor opportunity to shape both the products and the company culture.

How does remote work actually work?

We're async-first, meaning we don't expect you to be online at specific hours. We use Slack, Notion, and GitHub for communication. We have optional daily standups and weekly team calls. We value deep work and protect your focus time.

What's the team size?

We're a small team of under 10 people. Everyone knows everyone, and every person has a significant impact on what we build and how we work.

Do you sponsor visas?

We're exploring visa sponsorship options for exceptional candidates. Let us know your situation and we'll work through it together.
